Hello! I'm a PhD candidate in Information Sciences at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where I'm advised by Nigel Bosch and Jana Diesner. Prior to starting my PhD, I completed my B.S. in Mathematics and Statistics at UIUC.

I study how people engage in self-regulated learning and interact with AI-powered systems that support these strategies in computer-based learning environments. The central question that guides my research is: How can we leverage AI to help humans become better self-regulated learners?

I approach this question through three complementary research goals:
1. Modeling self-regulated learning in computer-based learning environments using learning analytics
2. Designing and developing AI systems that leverage eXplainable AI to support self-regulated learning
3. Evaluating these systems to ensure fairness, particularly for neurodiverse learners (e.g., individuals diagnosed with ADHD).
