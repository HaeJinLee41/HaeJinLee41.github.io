I am interested in developing an information system that utilizes Natural Language Processing and behavior change theories to promote preventive health behaviors. The title of my dissertation is Promoting a Healthy and Comprehensive Diet through Theory-driven Large Language Models-based Agents.

I am in the final year of my PhD in Information Sciences at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) and hold a bachelor's degree in Sociology with a minor in Digital Humanities from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA).

Project Overview

Representations of Health and Wellness on Instagram [video] [pdf]
Collaborators: Hunter Priniski, Keith Holyoak
[Project in Progress] This research assesses and models health information on Instagram, and discusses how the resulting model can be leveraged to build a scalable intervention that reduces mental health risks of some health influencers' posting behaviors.

Social Media in Psychotherapy [video] [Care Code] [scenario cards]
Collaborators: Center for Transcultural Psychiatry, FPC de Oostvaarderskliniek
[Project in Progress] We aim to design a culturally sensitive conversation keeper (e.g., Digital Storytelling Workshop) that captures the understanding of the patients on their treatment trajectory. Our goal is to bridge the gap between the care provided by the Center for Transcultural Psychiatry and patients with complex psychiatric problems.
